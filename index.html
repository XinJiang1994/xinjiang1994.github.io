BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

I received my B.Sc., M.Sc. and Ph.D degrees in computer science from Central South University, China, in 2017, 2020 and 2025 respectively. I was fortunate to have Academician Yaozhong Zhang as my advisor during both my master's and Ph.D. studies. During my Ph.D. studies, I served as a visiting Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong for one year and at Tsinghua University for three years. My collaborative supervisors were Professor Guoliang Xing and Academician Shimin Hu.

My research interests include image generation, video understanding, edge intelligence, and operating system.
